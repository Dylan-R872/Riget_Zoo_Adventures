Conservation at RZA


At Riget Zoo Adventures, our mission goes beyond unforgettable animal encounters and experiences. We are deeply committed to protecting endangered species, preserving natural habitats and inspiring every visitor to become a wildlife champion. Every ticket you purchase helps support conservation projects around the world.



How We Make a Difference


Wildlife Protection


We proudly support global programs that rescue, rehabilitate, and re-release endangered animals into the wild

Habitat Preservation


Through our 5 global partnerships, we help protect forests, oceans, and grasslands that animals depend on to survive

Breeding for the Future


Our ethical breeding programs focus on increasing our 12 supported vulnerable species and maintaining a natural genetic diversity

Education & Awareness


Our guided tours, school programs, and interactive displays teach visitors how small actions can make a big impact

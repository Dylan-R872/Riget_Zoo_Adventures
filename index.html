Welcome To Riget Zoo Adventures


Where wildlife, family fun, and conservation come together. Since our founding, our mission has been simple: to create unforgettable animal experiences while protecting the natural world for future generations. Today, Riget Zoo Adventures is home to hundreds of animals, immersive attractions, and a growing global conservation impact.



Our Mission:

To inspire a love of wildlife through education, adventure, and responsible animal care.


Our Vision:

A future where people and wildlife thrive together in balance.


Our Values:

Conservation, Education, Animal Welfare, Family Fun and Sustainability.


Protecting 120+ Animal Species

12 Conservation Programs Supported

5 Global Wildlife Partnerships

500,000+ Annual Visitors



Responsibility & Sustainability


We are committed to operating responsibly through sustainable practices, ethical animal care, waste reduction, and eco-friendly sourcing across all our facilities. Every decision we make is guided by the wellbeing of animals, people, and the planet.





Our Facilities


Petting Zoo


We proudly support global programs that rescue, rehabilitate, and re-release endangered animals into the wild

On-site Hotel


Through our 5 global partnerships, we help protect forests, oceans, and grasslands that animals depend on to survive

Family Amusements


Our ethical breeding programs focus on increasing our 12 supported vulnerable species and maintaining a natural genetic diversity

Restaurant & Gift Shop
